Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2026

What We Collect

We collect your email address and username when you create an account. That's used for account management only — logging in, resetting your password, that sort of thing.

We store your device's push notification token so we can send you alerts about weighbridge status changes for bridges you've favourited. If you don't enable notifications, we don't collect a token.

We store community content you submit — incident reports, bridge status updates, and comments. This content is visible to all users of the app.

We may collect anonymous, non-identifiable usage data (e.g. app crashes, feature usage) to help improve the app. This is not tied to your account.

What We Don't Do

We do not sell your data. We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We are not in the data business.

How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored securely using Supabase and transmitted via HTTPS. We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no system is perfect — use the app at your own risk.

Subscription & Payments

Payments are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not handle or store your payment information. Any billing queries should go through your Apple or Google account.

We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives a non-identifiable user ID to verify your subscription — no personal details are shared.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app means you accept the current version. We're not going to send you an email every time we change a word.
