YEAH COPY
PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: March 2026

The Short Version
Yeah Copy collects the minimum amount of data needed to run the app. We don't sell your data, we don't share it with advertisers, and we don't do anything dodgy with it. Simple as that.

Who We Are
Yeah Copy is an independent app built for Australian truck drivers. It is operated by Danny Wynn (Shamma Llama). For any privacy questions, contact us via Discord at discord.gg/mKXkmfMt.

What Data We Collect
Email address — used to create and log in to your account
Username — the name you choose to display in the app
Reports you submit — weighbridge status reports tied to your account
Report activity — confirms and disputes you make on other reports
Flags — if your account is flagged for suspicious reporting behaviour
What We Don't Collect
We do not collect your real name
We do not collect your location or GPS data
We do not collect your phone number
We do not track you across other apps or websites
We do not sell your data to anyone
We do not serve targeted advertisements
How We Use Your Data
Your data is used solely to operate the Yeah Copy app. This includes:

Authenticating your account when you log in
Displaying your username on reports and the leaderboard
Tracking report accuracy to maintain community trust
Identifying and restricting accounts that submit false reports
Data Storage
Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud database provider. Data is stored on servers in Australia where possible. Supabase's privacy policy can be found at supabase.com/privacy.

Account Restrictions
If your account is found to be submitting false or misleading reports, it may be restricted to "View Only" mode. This decision is made by the app administrator. Flagged activity (such as rapid status changes) is logged and reviewed manually.

Your Rights
You have the right to:

Request a copy of the data we hold about you
Request deletion of your account and associated data
Update your username at any time within the app
To exercise any of these rights, contact us on Discord at discord.gg/mKXkmfMt.

Children
Yeah Copy is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has created an account, contact us and we will remove it.

Changes to This Policy
If we make significant changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
